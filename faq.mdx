  <Accordion title="Does my company need to comply with LFPDPPP?">
    The Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de los Particulares (LFPDPPP) applies to any private-sector organization in Mexico that collects, uses, stores, or transfers personal data. This includes companies of all sizes operating in any industry.

    You are required to comply if your organization:

    - Collects personal data from clients, employees, suppliers, or any individual
    - Stores personal data in physical or digital form
    - Shares personal data with third parties

    Non-compliance can result in economic sanctions, reputational damage, and loss of customer trust. ByteWise offers a dedicated LFPDPPP compliance assessment to determine your current standing and identify exactly what needs to be corrected, including privacy notices, data handling policies, and staff training.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is penetration testing and how often should we do it?">
    Penetration testing (pentesting) is a controlled simulation of a cyberattack against your systems, networks, or applications. ByteWise's certified specialists attempt to exploit vulnerabilities the same way a real attacker would — before a real attacker does.

    A penetration test typically includes:

    - **Network and infrastructure testing** — Scanning for exposed services, misconfigurations, and exploitable entry points
    - **Application testing** — Identifying injection flaws, authentication weaknesses, and access control issues in web or internal systems
    - **Social engineering evaluation** — Testing whether employees can be manipulated into disclosing credentials or granting unauthorized access
    - **Hardening recommendations** — A prioritized report of findings with remediation guidance

    **Recommended frequency:** At minimum once per year, and additionally after significant infrastructure changes, new application deployments, or following any security incident.
  </Accordion>
