ByteWise designs and implements integrated cybersecurity strategies aligned to international standards, including ISO/IEC 27001:2022. Every engagement starts with a free initial diagnostic — a structured assessment of your current security posture, existing gaps, risk exposure, and the highest-priority actions your organization should take next.

## Free initial diagnostic

Before any paid engagement begins, ByteWise conducts a no-cost assessment that covers:

- Current state of your information security controls
- Identification of gaps relative to ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- Risk profile and exposure areas
- Prioritized list of recommended actions

<Tip>
  The diagnostic takes place in a single working session and produces a written summary. No purchase is required to receive it.
</Tip>

## What the service includes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Maturity assessment and gap analysis">
    ByteWise evaluates the current maturity of your information security program against the ISO/IEC 27001:2022 control framework, identifying specific gaps that must be addressed before certification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Critical asset identification and classification">
    Your information assets — systems, data, processes, and physical infrastructure — are catalogued and classified by criticality, enabling risk treatment decisions grounded in business context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Incident response plan">
    ByteWise develops a documented incident response plan tailored to your organization, covering detection, containment, eradication, recovery, and post-incident review procedures.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Controls, policies, and procedures implementation">
    The team implements the ISO 27001 Annex A controls applicable to your scope, along with the supporting information security policies and operational procedures required for the management system.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Staff training and security awareness">
    ByteWise delivers training sessions to build security awareness across your organization — covering social engineering, acceptable use, incident reporting, and role-specific responsibilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="ISO/IEC 27001 certification support">
    For organizations pursuing formal certification, ByteWise accompanies you through the internal audit cycle and coordinates readiness for the external certification audit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
