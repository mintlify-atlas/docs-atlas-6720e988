ByteWise supports your organization in meeting the requirements of the *Ley Federal de Protección de Datos Personales en Posesión de los Particulares* (LFPDPPP) — Mexico's federal law governing the handling of personal data by private-sector entities. Compliance is not only a legal obligation; it is a practical requirement for maintaining customer trust and avoiding regulatory sanctions.

## Why compliance matters

<Note>
  Inadequate handling of personal data can result in significant financial penalties, reputational damage, and loss of customers. The INAI (Instituto Nacional de Transparencia, Acceso a la Información y Protección de Datos Personales) actively audits and sanctions organizations that fail to meet the law's requirements.
</Note>

The most common compliance failures include missing or deficient privacy notices, absence of documented data handling procedures, and staff who have not been trained on their obligations under the law. Each of these exposes your organization to formal sanction proceedings.
