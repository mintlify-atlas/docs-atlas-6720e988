## Design and development approach

<Steps>
  <Step title="Requirements and objectives definition">
    ByteWise works with your stakeholders to document the functional requirements, technical constraints, integration needs, and success criteria for the project before any design or development begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Architecture and design">
    ByteWise produces a solution architecture and, for user-facing systems, interactive design prototypes — reviewed and approved by your team before development starts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Iterative development">
    Development proceeds in defined milestones with regular check-ins and deliverables for your review. Your feedback shapes each iteration before moving to the next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Testing and security review">
    Every deliverable undergoes functional testing and a security review before handoff. ByteWise does not separate security from development — it is built into the process from the start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deployment and documentation">
    ByteWise deploys the final solution to your production environment and provides technical documentation so your team understands what was built, how it works, and how to manage it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ongoing support">
    Post-launch, ByteWise is available for maintenance, feature additions, and updates — keeping the system current as your business and its technology context evolve.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Design principles

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Scalability" icon="arrow-up-right-dots" defaultOpen={true}>
    ByteWise designs systems to grow with your organization. Architecture decisions account for increased load, additional users, and new features — so the solution you receive today does not become a bottleneck tomorrow.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Security by design" icon="shield-check">
    Security is addressed at the architecture and development stage, not bolted on afterward. This includes input validation, authentication and authorization design, dependency management, and secure deployment configuration.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Business alignment" icon="bullseye-arrow">
    Technology decisions are made in service of your business objectives, not the other way around. ByteWise selects the tools and platforms that are the best fit for your context — not those that happen to be fashionable or convenient for the development team.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
