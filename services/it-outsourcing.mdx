## What ByteWise manages

Depending on your engagement scope, IT outsourcing with ByteWise can cover:

- Infrastructure management (servers, networking, cloud environments)
- Endpoint management and device configuration
- User account and access management
- Cybersecurity monitoring and incident response
- Software updates, patching, and vulnerability management
- Help desk and technical support for your team
- Vendor coordination and technology procurement guidance
- IT documentation and asset inventory

## How the engagement works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Technology inventory and assessment">
    ByteWise begins by documenting your existing infrastructure, identifying gaps, risks, and immediate improvement opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Service scope definition">
    Together with your team, ByteWise defines the specific services, response times, and responsibilities that form the basis of the engagement.
