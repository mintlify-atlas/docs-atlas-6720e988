## How ByteWise implements AI

Every ByteWise AI engagement follows a structured process that connects business objectives to working solutions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assess">
    ByteWise reviews your current workflows, data sources, and technology stack to identify high-impact automation and AI opportunities. This diagnostic is free and takes approximately 30 minutes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Design">
    Based on the assessment, ByteWise proposes a scoped solution: which processes to automate, which tools and models to use, how the solution integrates with your existing systems, and what success looks like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Implement">
    ByteWise builds and deploys the AI solution in phases, maintaining close coordination with your team throughout. Integrations, training data, and user testing are handled within the engagement.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optimize">
    After deployment, ByteWise monitors performance, refines models, and adjusts workflows as your organization evolves. Continuous accompaniment — not a one-time delivery — is the standard engagement model.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The free 30-minute AI diagnostic covers your automation readiness, existing data assets, and the highest-priority use cases for your organization. No technical preparation is required on your end.
</Tip>
